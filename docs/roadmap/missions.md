# Missions & Living Grid

**Status:** engine + authoring + play loop SHIPPED (code-verified 2026-06-11); reward sinks / discovery / multiplayer surface are the open gaps
**Depends on:** Checks, Mechanics (Challenges), Conditions, Areas, Instances, Traits, Skills, Distinctions, Societies, NPC Services

## Overview
Missions are branching narrative quest chains ‚Äî the primary way characters interact with the living world. A character receives a mission with broad objectives, makes decisions at branching points gated by different skills and traits, and the consequences reshape the world around them.

## Key Design Points (current architecture)
- **Mission = authored graph**: `MissionTemplate` ‚Üí `MissionNode` (pick-list of options) ‚Üí `MissionOption` (BRANCH or CHECK) ‚Üí `MissionOptionRoute` (outcome-tier-keyed, optionally randomized via weighted `Candidate`s). No engine arbitration ‚Äî the player picks, pick+check routes.
- **Two option sources**: authored predicate-gated options, and CHALLENGE-source options that reference one `mechanics.ChallengeTemplate` and fan out per qualifying `ChallengeApproach`.
- **Gating is predicate-trees** (predicate evaluator + leaf-resolver registry), not the older Application+Property eligibility sketch.
- **Front doors**: NPC givers via the unified `NPCServiceOffer(kind=MISSION)` (#686), or trigger givers (`MissionGiver`: ROOM_TRIGGER on entry / ENVIRONMENTAL_DETAIL on examine ‚Äî NPC-giver kind retired in favor of NPC services).
- **No scratch state**: state = node position + per-entry `MissionNodeSnapshot` + real consequences already applied.
- **Legend split**: Legend *Points* are mechanical; the Legend *Entry* (player retelling) is narrative and never parsed for mechanics.
- **Cooperative**: participant set on the instance; moral consequence follows the actor; contract-holder alone bears cooldown/standing/failure.
- **NOT using the Flows system** ‚Äî missions own their specific behavior.

## What Exists (code-verified 2026-06-11)
- **Engine (`world/missions/`), Phases 0‚Äì5b + play loop**: full model graph, predicate gating, resolution (`resolve_option` ‚Üí check ‚Üí tier routing ‚Üí consequence), multi-participant orchestrator (COINFLIP/VOTE/JOINT with combined routing ‚Äî engine only, see gaps), terminal reward emission + deferred-payout cron, renown award emission (LIVE ‚Äî fires `fire_renown_award`), Mission‚ÜíBeat seam (record-stub).
- **Quest journal + in-progress persistence (#889)**: `/api/missions/journal/` + `JournalPage`/`BeatCard` React play surface; node‚Üíroom binding (ANYWHERE/ANCHOR/ROOMS) with compass disclosure; durable deed history.
- **Visibility (#870)**: `MissionTemplate.visibility` (`OPEN`/`RESTRICTED`) ‚Äî **the old `access_tier` name is retired**; predicate-evaluated via `template_visible_to`.
- **Authoring (Phases A‚ÄìE + create UI all merged)**: Mission Studio (`/staff/missions/`) ‚Äî browser, create-from-scratch, node/option/route/candidate/reward CRUD, canvas DAG editor, predicate builder, copy/copy-subtree, staff `assign` power. Trigger-giver dispatch + staff editor (#863/#868); NPC roles + offers editor (#861 core, #1131 mission-details). #728 residuals: the role editor now exposes `is_active` (the one regression vs the deleted D3 giver pages) and the offer card surfaces the secondary knobs (rapport Œî success/failure, check type/difficulty, offer cooldown). The PERMIT-offer details panel remains a follow-up.
- **Deed knowledge (#902)**: mission-terminal awards grant the party witness knowledge of the deeds born from the run (bystanders deliberately excluded).
- **After-action reporting (#1753)**: money is delivered at the *report* step, not at resolution. A run with a report target reaches `RESOLVED` at resolution (`current_node=None`, money withheld) and pauses; the reporter returns to a co-located report-to **Functionary** (`MissionTemplate.report_to_role`, falling back to the giver's role; #1766) and issues `mission report <id> <style>` / `POST /api/missions/journal/<id>/report/`, which delivers the styled payout and transitions `RESOLVED ‚Üí COMPLETE`. A run with no report target completes directly at resolution (legend spreads, no coin). **Styles** (`ReportStyle`, `services/report.py`): Humble (+1 Bene, lower fame/prestige, baseline money), Accurate (promised money + fame/prestige), Embellished (a charm+Persuasion+Manipulation-spec manipulation check vs the giver ‚Äî success doubles money, raises fame/prestige, +1 Insidia; offered only with Persuasion). Legend stays flat across styles. Mostly-accurate is live (#1765): a PROVISIONAL Persuasion dodge check ‚Äî success skips the criminal consequences (heat + society sting), failure applies them, and the fame swing never grants either way. Reporting a masked run barefaced risks the association chance (the mask's heat copying to the reporting persona). Magnitudes are PLACEHOLDER.
- **Player tales (#2047)**: every participant of a terminal-status run can write their own free-text epilogue (tale) via `mission tale <id> <text>` (telnet) or `POST /api/missions/journal/{id}/tale/` (web). Permissive-canonicity policy (ADR-0105): canon by default, braggadocio is in-world texture, never parsed, never gated. On legend-minting runs, the tale seeds the author's `LegendDeedStory` for unstoried entries linked to the run's deeds. `MissionDeedRecord.legend_entries` M2M (populated at renown emission) is the prerequisite linkage.
- **Support moves (#2046 ‚úÖ shipped)**: a co-located participant can declare a flavored action fanned from their own capabilities (ownership oracle + predicate-tree legs) at a group beat, in place of their pick/vote. The helper rolls their own check; success banks an easing injected into the resolving check via `perform_check(extra_modifiers=...)`, failure can fire a complication via the consequence pipeline. Density comes from a `MissionAssistPattern` catalog (auto-offers where context + qualifier match; authored node gems add to or suppress). Discovery: qualifying moves light up; `rumored` moves show a veiled party-wide tease; the journal shows an unseen-approaches count after resolution. See ADR-0106.

## Open Gaps (from the 2026-06-10 code audit; #923 economy work closes the money one)
- **Reward sinks**: money now lands for real at the report step (#1753 ‚Äî `apply_deed_rewards` delivers via `deliver_mission_money`). **CRIME_WATCH is live** (#1765 ‚Äî `integrations/crime_watch.flag_crime` mints pursuit heat + the society-reputation sting via the justice app; `ref` carries the `CrimeKind` slug). Beat records in-memory; rumor remains a stub pending tidings. Renown is already real. Criminality is declared at deed birth: renown emission tags every minted legend entry with the run's CRIME_WATCH kinds, so tellings of a mission deed mint heat wherever a law matches.
- **No discovery surface**: ‚úÖ **shipped** (#2044) ‚Äî `GiverKind.BOARD` is a third giver-kind: an examinable object that lists many eligible postings (preview-then-take). Examine renders a numbered list; `mission take <n>` (telnet) and `POST /api/missions/boards/<pk>/take/` (web) re-run eligibility before granting via the same `staff_assign_mission` path trigger dispatch uses. `GET /api/missions/journal/opportunities/` + the journal opportunities tab surface three groups (here / nearby / your-organizations); trigger givers show flavor only (never their pool ‚Äî would spoil the draw). Telnet `mission opportunities` mirrors the tab. Discovery is world-anchored (ADR-0094): boards/givers are the doors; the web tab is a map, not a door.
- **Abandon**: #1023 ‚úÖ shipped ‚Äî `play.abandon_mission` + `MissionJournalViewSet.abandon` let the contract holder walk away from an `ACTIVE` run (‚Üí `ABANDONED`, frees the cap slot, keeps the giver cooldown). Auto-**expiry** was intentionally dropped (missions are private; persisting until abandoned is fine) ‚Äî `EXPIRED` stays a defined-but-unused status for a possible future GM action.
- **Multiplayer group play**: #1036 ‚úÖ shipped (backend slice of #887) ‚Äî `ConflictMode` collapsed to **GROUP_VOTE** (converge: two-stage pick‚Üívote, plurality, random tie) vs **JOINT** (parallel). `MissionGroupBallot` + `play.submit_group_pick`/`cast_group_vote`/`group_beat` + the `journal/{id}/group-pick|group-vote|group-beat` API drive `multiplayer.resolve_group_node`; resolution fires on all-voted (GROUP_VOTE) / all-picked (JOINT) or a 120s window (lazy-on-access + `cron.resolve_expired_group_votes` backstop). **#887 invite handshake ‚úÖ shipped**: `MissionInvite` RSVP model + `invite_to_mission`/`respond_to_mission_invite` services (accept ‚Üí `share_mission`); telnet `mission invite <id> <name>` / `mission accept|decline <invite-id>` + web `POST /api/missions/journal/<id>/invite/` + `POST /api/missions/journal/respond/`. **Per-actor group narrative ‚úÖ shipped**: `resolve_group_node` emits per-actor STORY + a room-wide ambient stir. **Group-path location conjunct ‚úÖ shipped**: `multiplayer.build_group_option_list` applies `option_is_locally_live` per participant. **Frontend group beat card + invite flow ‚úÖ shipped** (#2049): `GroupBeatCard`, `InvitePicker`, `PendingInvitesSection`; `character_name` on `GroupBallotState`, `pending_invites` + `participant_count` on the journal payload.
- **Telnet play surface**: #1349 ‚úÖ shipped ‚Äî `CmdMission` (`commands/missions.py`) is a thin telnet face over the same `services.play` functions the web `MissionJournalViewSet` calls (no separate Action): `mission`/`mission beat <id>` (journal + numbered beat options), `mission resolve <id> <n>`, `mission abandon <id>`, the two-stage `mission pick`/`mission vote` group decision, `mission report <id> <style>` (#1753 ‚Äî the after-action payout at a report-to Functionary), and `mission invite <id> <name>` / `mission accept|decline <invite-id>` (#887 ‚Äî the co-op invite handshake). Pending invites surface in bare `mission`/`mission list`. This makes the mission play loop drivable as a pure-backend E2E journey test (`integration_tests/pipeline/test_missions_telnet_e2e.py`), the #1328 goal. GM-authoring surfaces (Mission Studio) stay web/admin-only.
- **Per-candidate overrides ‚úÖ shipped**: per-candidate consequence/reward emission is wired (`rewards.emit_candidate_rewards`, called from `resolution.resolve_option` and `multiplayer.resolve_group_node`); the mission roulette reveal #933 couples to it (`_emit_candidate_roulette` spins the candidate draw).
- **Instanced play ‚úÖ shipped**: `spawn_instanced_room` wiring is live (#886) ‚Äî `resolution.py` spawns the run's instanced room on the resolving option and `play.py` tears it down via `complete_instanced_room`. Prison/ransom #931 is its flagship consumer.

The legacy org-treasury `demand_ransom`/`pay_ransom` Contract path was **retired** ‚Äî the project is the single ransom route.
