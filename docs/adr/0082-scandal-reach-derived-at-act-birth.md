# 0082 — Scandal is a derived per-society judgment; reach forks at act birth

**Status:** accepted (2026-07-03, #1464; settles #1450 Q1+Q2)

Public reaction is knowledge propagation (the #1765 heat shape), and *scandal*
is not a taxonomy: an act is scandalous **to a society** when its archetype
vectors dot-producted against that society's six principles fall below a
threshold — the same product reputation-on-awareness already computes.
Adultery, oath-breaking, or dullness-to-the-Nox are archetype data rows, never
code; the archetype library is therefore load-bearing vocabulary and deed
sources (mission options, encounter outcomes, story beats) must tag it.

Reach is **derived at deed birth**, never authored: room privacy forks the
act (private → a contained, act-anchored `Secret` on the actor — blackmail
material inside the mystery loop; public → the realm walk's societies become
aware), a containment check (the actor's best social tool against the crowd
size) routes the scandalous-public case, and spread extent scales with the
fame of those involved via `spread_multiplier`. Continental/world are never
minted — they remain escalation (gemit, the common-knowledge threshold,
legend). Concealment has two moments: Stealth/magic at the act decide who
witnesses at all; social containment afterwards decides whether witnesses
talk. A perfectly concealed act still mints its deed and Secret — the truth
exists and is discoverable; there is simply no initial knowledge to spread.

**Rejected:** a static place→stakeholder attachment model (a far society
isn't indifferent — word never reached them); an authored per-act reach
field (derivation keeps authoring honest and reach dramatic); a separate
"public event" object or outrage meter (awareness + reputation + the
leak→heat composition are the reaction); magnitude-only reach for scene
deeds (who was involved matters more than how big the deed rated).
